# Product Wedge

## Wedge statement

No trade without evidence. No execution without risk approval.

## Product definition

A professional-grade personal trading operating system for one serious operator.

It helps the operator:

- define a strategy
- test it on historical data
- inspect trade evidence
- measure risk
- compare strategy versions
- classify strategy maturity
- paper trade safely
- approve or reject execution
- monitor live behavior only after promotion

## What it is not

It is not:

- a guaranteed-profit AI bot
- a signal-selling product
- a Telegram-style trade alert system
- a leverage machine
- a social sentiment casino
- a high-frequency trading system
- a public SaaS product in the early phases

## First wedge implementation

The first implementation is a Strategy Backtesting + Risk Review Dashboard.

It must answer:

- What strategy was tested?
- What data was used?
- Were fees and slippage included?
- What trades happened and why?
- What was the max drawdown?
- What are the failure conditions?
- Is this strategy rejected, research-only, paper-trading-ready, or live-candidate?
