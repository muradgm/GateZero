# Market Intelligence Truth

TraderFrame may read market data, news, events, red flags, and strategy signals in future phases.

It may produce evidence-based scenarios and risk-gated action candidates for operator review.

It must not present predictions as certainty, issue unreviewed live buy/sell commands, promise
profit, bypass risk review, or move from scenario to action without explicit operator decision.

## Required Intelligence Output Fields

Every market-intelligence output must include:

- evidence
- source references
- confidence level
- red flags
- invalidation condition
- risk review status
- operator decision requirement

## Allowed Future Intelligence Work

- Market condition summaries.
- Related news and event scanning.
- Red-flag detection.
- Signal candidate analysis.
- Evidence-based market scenarios.
- Risk-gated watch, reject, paper-simulate, or prepare-plan recommendation drafts.

## Blocked Intelligence Work

- Prediction certainty.
- Profit promises.
- Unreviewed trade recommendations.
- Live buy/sell command generation.
- Autonomous execution.
- Hidden promotion from scenario to action.

## Product Boundary

Market intelligence exists to improve evidence, risk framing, and operator decision quality.

It does not replace risk review, operator authority, strategy testing, or execution controls.
