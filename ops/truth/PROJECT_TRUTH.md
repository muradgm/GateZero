# Project Truth

## Product identity

This product is a GateZero-controlled trading intelligence, research, risk-control, and
execution-support system for one serious operator.

It is not an AI money machine. It does not promise profit. It does not assume prediction is possible
or reliable.

## Core purpose

The system exists to help the operator:

- read market conditions
- inspect related news and events
- detect red flags
- analyze signal candidates
- research strategies honestly
- backtest strategies reproducibly
- detect weak assumptions and data leakage
- measure risk before execution
- paper trade before live trading
- maintain full evidence for every decision
- prevent unsafe execution
- stop trading when risk limits are breached

## Current product truth

The first real product is a backtesting and strategy-risk review workspace.

A strategy may be created, tested, reviewed, and classified, but it may not place real trades.

Future market-intelligence work may summarize conditions, source events, red flags, signal
candidates, and evidence-based scenarios only when outputs remain source-linked, uncertainty-aware,
risk-gated, and operator-reviewed.

## Hard constraint

Engineering quality does not create market edge.

A well-built system can still lose money if the strategy has no edge, the data is misleading, the
execution model is unrealistic, or the operator overrides risk controls.

## Promotion rule

No strategy may move toward live execution unless:

1. Strategy logic is tested.
2. Backtest results are reproducible.
3. Fees and slippage are included.
4. Lookahead bias checks pass.
5. Max drawdown is visible.
6. Risk limits are configured.
7. Paper trading has produced sufficient evidence.
8. Risk Officer approves.
9. QA/Security approves.
10. PM accepts the product and operator readiness.
11. Human operator explicitly approves.

## Product value

The product value is discipline, evidence, risk control, auditability, and execution safety.

The product value is not prediction hype.
