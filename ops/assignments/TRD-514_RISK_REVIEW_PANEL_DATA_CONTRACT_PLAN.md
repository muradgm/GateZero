# TRD-514 Risk-Review Panel Data Contract Plan

Status: accepted

## Goal

Plan a data contract for a risk-review panel that remains separate from approval.

## Scope

- Represent risk checks, blockers, assumptions, and limitation references.
- Keep validation as repository evidence.
- Keep risk review distinct from permission or readiness.

## Blocked Scope

- Approval scoring, readiness scoring, performance ranking, risk-gate loosening, and execution
  authorization.

## Acceptance

Accepted when risk data is framed as review evidence only.
