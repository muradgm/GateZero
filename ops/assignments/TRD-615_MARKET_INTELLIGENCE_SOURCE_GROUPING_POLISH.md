# TRD-615 Market Intelligence Source Grouping Polish

Status: accepted

## Goal

Group scenario sources by operating purpose for faster local inspection.

## Scope

- Separate scenario inputs, risk controls, and local provenance.
- Keep every source reference visible and checked in.

## Blocked Scope

- Remote source retrieval, external output, hidden compaction, accounts, credentials, and actions.

## Acceptance

Accepted when the operator can distinguish input, control, and provenance sources at a glance.
