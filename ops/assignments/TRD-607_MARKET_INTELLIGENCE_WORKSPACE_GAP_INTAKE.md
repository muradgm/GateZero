# TRD-607 Market Intelligence Workspace Gap Intake

Status: accepted

## Goal

Identify the next concrete product gap after the first read-only market workspace batch.

## Scope

- Inspect the rendered workspace, data model, runtime refresh, and tests.
- Select only a gap that improves operator inspection within Gate 2 planning.

## Blocked Scope

- External accounts, credentials, live routes, autonomous action, final recommendations, claims,
  output channels, promotion semantics, and risk-gate changes.

## Acceptance

Accepted when the gap is concrete, implementation-facing, and bounded to local scenario inspection.
