# TRD-393 Gate 1 Closeout Signoff Packet

## Goal

Prepare Gate 1 closeout signoff criteria without moving gates.

## Scope

- Define signoff requirements for the current Gate 1 lane.
- Keep signoff criteria evidence-based and locally verifiable.
- Preserve Gate 1 as the active operating gate.

## Blocked

- No Gate 2 activation.
- No provider credentials, broker integration, paper/live execution, autonomy, or AI prediction.
- No strategy approval, readiness, profitability, or execution permission.

## Acceptance

- Signoff packet exists and is source-linked.
- Gate movement remains blocked.
- `pnpm verify:gate0` passes.
