# TRD-544 Evidence Detail Risk Adjacency Pass

Status: accepted

## Goal

Keep limitations and reproducibility notes adjacent to evidence detail.

## Scope

- Add a local adjacency block for reproducibility notes and limitations.
- Ensure the evidence detail does not read as permission to trade.

## Blocked Scope

- Readiness, approval, safety, deployability, profitability, or promotion language.

## Acceptance

Accepted when tests confirm evidence detail appears before broader limitations and risk sections.
