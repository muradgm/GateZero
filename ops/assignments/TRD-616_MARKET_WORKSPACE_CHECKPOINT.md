# TRD-616 Market Workspace Checkpoint

Status: accepted

## Goal

Decide whether a meaningful product gap remains after workspace hardening.

## Scope

- Review empty states, consistency, runtime preservation, mobile and keyboard behavior, copy, and
  source grouping.
- Identify the next gated product milestone without changing the active scope.

## Blocked Scope

- Scope promotion, external accounts, credentials, live routes, autonomous action, and claims.

## Acceptance

Accepted when the workspace lane is closed and the next milestone begins with explicit risk
authorization.
