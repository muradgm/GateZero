# TRD-405 Material Gap Intake Result

## Goal

Record whether any material Gate 1 maintenance gap blocks the approved movement lane.

## Scope

- Review the accepted closeout, blocker, signoff, and verification records.
- Record whether a new Gate 1 gap requires rollback or pause.
- Keep the result as a local governance artifact.

## Blocked

- No strategy promotion.
- No execution implementation.
- No loosened scanner, risk, or autonomy gates.

## Acceptance

- Material gap intake result exists.
- Any gap is routed to rollback or maintenance.
- No blocking Gate 1 gap is silently bypassed.
