# TRD-528 Frontend Evidence Detail Display Packet

Status: accepted

## Goal

Prepare a future frontend display packet for simulation evidence detail after contracts exist.

## Scope

- Read-only display only.
- Local evidence detail data.
- Risk and limitation adjacency.
- No implementation until contract guards are ready.

## Blocked Scope

- Account setup, credential input, execution controls, AI prediction, approval labels, readiness
  labels, and performance claims.

## Acceptance

Accepted when display work is explicitly blocked behind contract implementation and guard coverage.
