# TRD-471 Limitation Panel Copy Contract

## Goal

Define copy rules for future limitation panels so assumptions stay visible near evidence.

## Scope

- Gate: `G2_PAPER_TRADING`.
- Scope: `paper_simulation_planning_only`.
- Surface: limitation language, assumption language, caveat placement, and blocked claims.

## Blocked Scope

- No readiness, approval, safety, profitability, certification, deployment, or execution language.

## Required Outputs

- Limitation panel copy contract.
- QA/security, risk, and orchestrator review records.
- Tracker, docs index, command-center, and guard-index updates.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Limitations are specific and adjacent to evidence.
- Copy prevents simulation evidence from becoming trading permission.
- Next task remains risk panel copy contract.
