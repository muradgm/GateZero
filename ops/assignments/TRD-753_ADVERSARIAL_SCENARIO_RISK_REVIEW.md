# TRD-753 Adversarial Scenario Risk Review

Status: accepted

## Goal

Challenge scenario assumptions before operator consideration.

## Outputs

- Adversarial challenge and review contracts.
- Checks for source quality, timeframe conflict, counter-evidence, regime assumptions, invalidation,
  and semantic safety.

## Blocked Scope

No risk self-approval, scenario promotion, order preparation, or execution.

## Acceptance

Placeholder, unlinked, or untestable counter-evidence, regime assumptions, and invalidation criteria
create high-severity challenges. Any high or critical unresolved challenge blocks operator
consideration.
