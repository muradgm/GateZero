# TRD-399 Operator Decision Authority Review

## Goal

Confirm the operator remains final authority for decisions.

## Scope

- Review current decision authority language.
- Preserve human decision points.
- Keep automation advisory or evidence-only.

## Blocked

- No autonomous trading decisions.
- No strategy approval automation.
- No execution authority.

## Acceptance

- Operator decision authority review exists.
- Operator remains final authority.
- `pnpm verify:gate0` passes.
