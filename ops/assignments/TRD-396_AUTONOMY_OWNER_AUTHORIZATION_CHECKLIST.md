# TRD-396 Autonomy Owner Authorization Checklist

## Goal

Draft the autonomy-owner authorization checklist required before any future autonomy change.

## Scope

- Identify autonomy review requirements.
- Preserve operator decision authority.
- Keep all autonomy changes blocked.

## Blocked

- No autonomous execution.
- No AI buy/sell prediction.
- No autonomous order routing.

## Acceptance

- Autonomy-owner checklist exists.
- Autonomy remains unchanged.
- `pnpm verify:gate0` passes.
