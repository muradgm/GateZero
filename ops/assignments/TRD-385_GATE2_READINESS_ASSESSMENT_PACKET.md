# TRD-385 Gate 2 Readiness Assessment Packet

## Goal

Draft an assessment-only Gate 2 readiness packet without activating Gate 2.

## Scope

- Define what must be true before Gate 2 planning could start.
- Keep all conclusions as assessment or blockers.
- Preserve Gate 1 as the active gate.

## Blocked

- No Gate 2 activation.
- No paper/simulated execution implementation.
- No broker, credential, provider, autonomous execution, or AI prediction work.

## Acceptance

- Gate 2 readiness assessment packet exists.
- The packet explicitly says Gate 2 is not active.
- `pnpm verify:gate0` passes.
