# TRD-395 Risk Owner Authorization Checklist

## Goal

Draft the risk-owner authorization checklist required before any future gate movement.

## Scope

- Identify risk-owner review requirements.
- Keep requirements as prerequisites only.
- Preserve current risk gates.

## Blocked

- No risk-gate loosening.
- No execution, approval, readiness, or profitability language.

## Acceptance

- Risk-owner checklist exists.
- Risk controls remain equal or stricter.
- `pnpm verify:gate0` passes.
