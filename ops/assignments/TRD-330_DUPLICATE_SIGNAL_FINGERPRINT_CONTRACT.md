# TRD-330 Duplicate Signal Fingerprint Contract

## Goal

Draft the duplicate-signal fingerprint contract boundary before any deduplication logic is built.

## Scope

- Define fingerprint provenance expectations.
- Keep duplicate signals as blockers.
- Require no-claim, evidence-only language.

## Blocked

- No automated signal approval.
- No trade action generation.

## Acceptance

- Contract doc is source-linked and indexed.
- Reviews confirm fingerprinting remains local evidence hygiene only.
