# TRD-502 Frontend Evidence Detail Expansion Plan

Status: accepted

## Goal

Define how evidence detail may deepen later without creating action controls or strategy claims.

## Scope

- Keep evidence detail local and read-only.
- Keep limitations near evidence.
- Treat validation as repository health only.

## Blocked Scope

- Broker data, external accounts, credentials, live routes, autonomous execution, prediction
  prompts, approval wording, and profitability claims.

## Required Outputs

- Evidence detail planning record.
- QA_SECURITY review.
- RISK review.
- ORCHESTRATOR acceptance.

## Acceptance

Accepted when the plan narrows future work to local evidence display and no implementation exceeds
current gate scope.
