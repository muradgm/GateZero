# TRD-752 Conditional Scenario Synthesis

Status: accepted

## Goal

Create a balanced conditional scenario set from clear local evidence.

## Outputs

- Bullish, bearish, and neutral scenario contracts.
- All-three-or-none synthesis.
- Source-linked supporting evidence, counter-evidence, regime assumptions, observable invalidation,
  red flags, limitations, and bounded confidence.
- Evidence-chain identity and full rendered-text safety checks.

## Blocked Scope

No selected scenario, buy or sell instruction, sizing, timing, final recommendation, or claim.

## Acceptance

Clear, identity-matched, semantically safe evidence produces three non-actionable scenarios; blocked
evidence or unsafe draft text produces none.
