# TRD-751 Evidence And Semantic Quality Gate

Status: accepted

## Goal

Fail closed on weak evidence or unsafe intelligence text.

## Outputs

- Evidence-quality findings and disposition.
- Deterministic freshness from source as-of time and explicit maximum age.
- Structured provenance and mandatory local file-integrity validation.
- Deterministic semantic classifier.
- Automatic scanning of every timeframe evidence field.
- Blocked scenario consideration for every finding.
- Tests for direct and disguised instructions, certainty, profitability, ranking, and authority
  conflicts.

## Blocked Scope

No probabilistic trade scoring, censorship bypass, final recommendation, or action route.

## Acceptance

Stale, falsely fresh, missing, hash-mismatched, conflicting, weak-provenance, or semantically unsafe
input cannot reach scenario consideration.
