# TRD-366 Readiness Blocker Language Recheck

## Goal

Recheck and constrain readiness language after the adapter planning freeze.

## Scope

- Identify acceptable alternatives to readiness wording.
- Keep blocker language tied to evidence and local verification.
- Prevent validation success from becoming approval semantics.

## Blocked

- No strategy readiness labels.
- No approval, promotion, profitability, or trading permission language.
- No execution or broker capability.

## Acceptance

- Readiness blocker language recheck exists.
- Risk review confirms approval language remains clean.
- `pnpm verify:gate0` passes.
