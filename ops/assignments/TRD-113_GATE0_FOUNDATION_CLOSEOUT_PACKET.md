# TRD-113: Gate 0 Foundation Closeout Packet

## Objective

Summarize the completed Gate 0 foundation workstream without authorizing any later-phase work.

## Scope

Allowed:

- Summarize accepted Gate 0 foundation, evidence, validation, documentation, and tracker controls.
- Record current validation status and remaining boundary.
- Identify the next safe maintenance task.

Blocked:

- Any claim of trading readiness, strategy performance, operator approval, execution approval,
  product launch readiness, or gate movement.

## Required Output

- `docs/operations/GATE0_FOUNDATION_CLOSEOUT_PACKET.md`
- QA_SECURITY, RISK, and ORCHESTRATOR review records.
- Updated tracker and documentation indexes.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The closeout is local, source-linked, and non-authorizing.
- The closeout preserves Gate 0 Research Only.
- The tracklist moves to `TRD-113` with zero open accepted-packet gaps.
