# TRD-329 Parameter Hash Provenance Record

## Goal

Document how parameter hashes support provenance without implying strategy approval.

## Scope

- Describe expected and observed parameter hash roles.
- Keep parameter drift as a blocker.
- Preserve operator authority and risk review.

## Blocked

- No strategy promotion.
- No claim that matching hashes prove profitability or readiness.

## Acceptance

- Provenance doc is source-linked and indexed.
- Reviews confirm no approval semantics.
