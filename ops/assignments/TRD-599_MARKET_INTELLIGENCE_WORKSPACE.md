# TRD-599 Market Intelligence Workspace

Status: accepted

## Goal

Render sourced scenario intelligence in the read-only Strategy Review Workspace.

## Scope

- Gate 2 paper-simulation planning only.
- Local fixtures, contracts, tests, or read-only Command Center display as needed.
- Evidence, source references, limitation notes, risk review, and operator decision requirements
  stay visible.

## Blocked Scope

- Broker integration, external account routes, credentials, live execution, autonomous action, final
  recommendations, certainty claims, performance claims, output channels, approval semantics,
  readiness claims, and risk-gate loosening.

## Acceptance

Accepted when the implementation keeps the workspace local, evidence-only, risk-gated,
operator-reviewed, and free of action controls.
