# TRD-383 Gate 1 Closeout Evidence Review

## Goal

Review Gate 1 evidence to determine whether the current historical-backtesting-only lane is ready
for closeout assessment.

## Scope

- Review accepted Gate 1 evidence surfaces.
- Confirm local verification and review coverage.
- Keep this as assessment-only work.

## Blocked

- No Gate 2 activation.
- No provider credentials, broker integration, paper/live execution, autonomous execution, or AI
  buy/sell prediction.
- No strategy approval, readiness, or profitability claims.

## Acceptance

- Closeout evidence review exists.
- Evidence state is summarized without creating trading authority.
- `pnpm verify:gate0` passes.
