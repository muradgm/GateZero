# TRD-613 Scenario Copy Minimization Pass

Status: accepted

## Goal

Reduce repeated scenario copy while keeping limitations and review requirements visible.

## Scope

- Tighten summary, risk notes, simulation limitations, and blocked-scope reminders.
- Keep uncertainty, operator review, and no-action meaning explicit.

## Blocked Scope

- Promotional copy, certainty, performance claims, readiness language, and concealed limitations.

## Acceptance

Accepted when copy is shorter without weakening evidence, risk, or operator-control meaning.
