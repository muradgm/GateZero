# TRD-524 Risk-Review Panel Fixture Plan

Status: accepted

## Goal

Plan fixtures for future risk-review panel data.

## Scope

- Blocker records.
- Limitation records.
- Assumption references.
- Manual risk notes.

## Blocked Scope

- Approval scoring, readiness scoring, promotion language, risk-gate loosening, and execution
  authorization.

## Acceptance

Accepted when fixtures keep risk review separate from authorization.
