# TRD-373 Gate 1 Maintenance Gap Intake

## Goal

Identify whether any concrete Gate 1 maintenance gap remains after the latest maintenance
checkpoint.

## Scope

- Review tracker, command-center, review, scanner, and evidence-refresh maintenance surfaces.
- Keep findings as local operating evidence only.
- Preserve Gate 1 historical-backtesting-only scope.

## Blocked

- No Gate 2 activation.
- No broker, provider, credential, paper/live execution, or autonomous execution work.
- No strategy approval, readiness, or profitability claims.

## Acceptance

- Maintenance gap intake record exists.
- Any remaining work is queued as maintenance only.
- `pnpm verify:gate0` passes.
