# TRD-503 Frontend Limitation Copy Audit

Status: accepted

## Goal

Audit visible limitation copy for claim safety and operator clarity.

## Scope

- Confirm limitation copy stays near evidence.
- Confirm copy avoids readiness, approval, safety, profitability, and market-access claims.
- Preserve calm operational wording.

## Blocked Scope

- Marketing claims, performance claims, live trading claims, broker readiness, and approval
  semantics.

## Required Outputs

- Limitation copy audit.
- QA_SECURITY review.
- RISK review.
- ORCHESTRATOR acceptance.

## Acceptance

Accepted when limitation copy remains precise, non-promotional, and claim-safe.
