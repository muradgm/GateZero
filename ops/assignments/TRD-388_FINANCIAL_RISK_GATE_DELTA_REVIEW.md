# TRD-388 Financial Risk Gate Delta Review

## Goal

Identify financial-risk-gate deltas required before any future phase could be considered.

## Scope

- Review current risk-gate posture.
- Identify risk controls needed before next-phase planning.
- Keep this as assessment-only.

## Blocked

- No risk-gate loosening.
- No execution path.
- No strategy approval, readiness, or profitability claims.

## Acceptance

- Financial risk gate delta review exists.
- Risk controls remain equal or stricter.
- `pnpm verify:gate0` passes.
