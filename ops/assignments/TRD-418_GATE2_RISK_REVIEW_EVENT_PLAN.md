# TRD-418 Gate 2 Risk Review Event Plan

## Goal

Plan the future Gate 2 risk review event contract before simulated mechanics exist.

## Scope

- Define required risk-review evidence fields.
- Preserve operator decision authority.
- Require explicit blocking dispositions.

## Blocked

- No risk approval automation.
- No strategy promotion.
- No risk-gate loosening.
- No execution authority.

## Acceptance

- Risk review event plan exists.
- Reviews confirm risk events are control records only.
