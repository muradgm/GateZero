# TRD-387 Autonomy Gate Delta Review

## Goal

Identify autonomy-gate deltas required before any future phase can increase autonomy.

## Scope

- Review the current autonomy posture.
- Identify deltas as blockers only.
- Preserve operator decision authority.

## Blocked

- No autonomous execution.
- No autonomous order routing.
- No AI buy/sell prediction.
- No readiness or approval semantics.

## Acceptance

- Autonomy gate delta review exists.
- Autonomy remains unchanged.
- `pnpm verify:gate0` passes.
