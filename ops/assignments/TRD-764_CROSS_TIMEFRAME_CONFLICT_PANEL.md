# TRD-764 Cross-Timeframe Conflict Panel

Status: accepted

## Goal

Show whether hourly, daily, and monthly evidence conflicts and require revision when conflicts
exist.

## Output

- Conflict-panel contract, assembler, fixture, and workspace record.

## Acceptance

- No-conflict records remain neutral.
- Any recorded conflict blocks scenario consideration and requires revision.
