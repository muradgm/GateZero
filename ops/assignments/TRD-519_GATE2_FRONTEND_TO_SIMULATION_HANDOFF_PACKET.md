# TRD-519 Gate 2 Frontend-To-Simulation Handoff Packet

Status: accepted

## Goal

Define the handoff from the read-only frontend lane to local simulation evidence planning.

## Scope

- Route next work toward local simulation evidence contracts.
- Require QA/RISK review before any implementation.
- Keep frontend changes secondary to evidence trust.

## Blocked Scope

- Broker integration, live execution, autonomous execution, credentials, AI prediction, and strategy
  approval semantics.

## Acceptance

Accepted when the handoff identifies the next lane without authorizing blocked scope.
