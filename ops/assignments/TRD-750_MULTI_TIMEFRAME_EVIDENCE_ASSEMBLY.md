# TRD-750 Multi-Timeframe Evidence Assembly

Status: accepted

## Goal

Assemble hourly, daily, and monthly evidence without flattening disagreement.

## Outputs

- Strict timeframe evidence records.
- Exactly-one-per-timeframe assembly.
- Coverage, as-of, source, conflict, counter-evidence, risk, and limitation fields.

## Blocked Scope

No market direction selection, opportunity ranking, trading instruction, or external data.

## Acceptance

All three timeframes remain separate and every cross-timeframe conflict remains visible.
