# TRD-417 Simulated Fill Assumption Plan

## Goal

Plan future simulated-fill assumption records without making performance or readiness claims.

## Scope

- Define assumption categories for future simulation evidence.
- Require explicit uncertainty and limitation fields.
- Keep assumptions subordinate to risk review.

## Blocked

- No fill engine implementation.
- No profitability claims.
- No readiness language.
- No live market route.

## Acceptance

- Assumption plan exists.
- RISK review confirms assumptions remain limitations, not approvals.
