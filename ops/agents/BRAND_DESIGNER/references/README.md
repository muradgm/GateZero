# Brand Designer References

These references are required reading for the BRAND_DESIGNER agent. They keep GateZero brand work
grounded in product truth, risk discipline, and production-quality identity standards.

## Reference Policy

- Internal GateZero truth and governance files outrank visual preference.
- Brand work must not imply guaranteed profit, AI trading magic, execution authority, or strategy
  approval.
- If a reference conflicts with `ops/truth/RISK_RULES.md`, the stricter risk interpretation wins.
- References are for identity quality and communication discipline, not investment advice.
