**Project: TraderFrame — Evidence Gate**  
**Stage: Narrative Strategy**  
**Objective: Define the transformation from fragmented evidence to approved decision record**

---

### **1. Product Truth (Approved Upstream)**  
TraderFrame is a private trading decision-intelligence experience that transforms fragmented market evidence into explainable, risk-aware decisions. It is designed for independent traders, portfolio managers, and research-led market participants who demand transparency, control, and precision in their decision-making processes.

---

### **2. Audience**  
- **Independent Traders**: Seek to validate their intuition with data and structure.  
- **Portfolio Managers**: Need to justify decisions to stakeholders with clear, auditable reasoning.  
- **Research-Led Market Participants**: Require rigorous evidence filtering and transparent reasoning for their strategies.

---

### **3. Message Hierarchy**  
**Core Message**:  
> *Market conviction becomes credible when evidence is filtered, challenged, approved and recorded transparently.*

**Supporting Messages**:  
- **Evidence First**: Every decision starts with data, not intuition.  
- **Transparent Reasoning**: Every step is visible, auditable, and explainable.  
- **User Control**: You decide what to include, challenge, or exclude.  
- **Risk Awareness**: Every decision is evaluated for risk and impact.  
- **Calm Precision**: The process is methodical, focused, and grounded in logic.

---

### **4. Narrative Strategy: Transformation from Fragmented Evidence to Approved Decision Record**

#### **A. Opening: The Fragmented Evidence Landscape**  
- **Visual**: A chaotic, fragmented visual of market data — news, charts, sentiment, trade data, etc.  
- **Narrative**: "In the fast-paced world of trading, information is everywhere, but it’s often unstructured, conflicting, and unreliable."  
- **Emotion**: Frustration, uncertainty, and the risk of acting on incomplete data.

#### **B. The Evidence Gate: A Structured Process**  
- **Visual**: A 3D interface that represents the "Evidence Gate" — a central hub where data is filtered, challenged, and validated.  
- **Narrative**: "TraderFrame introduces the Evidence Gate, a structured process that transforms chaos into clarity. Here, every piece of evidence is evaluated, questioned, and validated before it becomes part of a decision."  
- **Emotion**: Control, confidence, and clarity.

#### **C. Filtering & Challenging: The First Gate**  
- **Visual**: A 3D filter wheel or sieve that separates relevant from irrelevant data.  
- **Narrative**: "The first gate filters out noise. It asks: Is this data relevant? Is it reliable? Does it support the hypothesis?"  
- **Emotion**: Precision, skepticism, and rigor.

#### **D. Approval & Record: The Final Gate**  
- **Visual**: A final confirmation panel with a "Record" button, showing the decision as a transparent, auditable record.  
- **Narrative**: "Once the evidence is validated, it is approved and recorded. This is your decision, documented with full transparency and risk awareness."  
- **Emotion**: Confidence, control, and accountability.

#### **E. The Outcome: A Transparent, Risk-Aware Decision**  
- **Visual**: A 3D timeline or dashboard showing the full decision process, from evidence to record.  
- **Narrative**: "TraderFrame turns fragmented evidence into a clear, explainable, and risk-aware decision. This is how you build trust — with your data, your reasoning, and your process."  
- **Emotion**: Trust, clarity, and mastery.

---
