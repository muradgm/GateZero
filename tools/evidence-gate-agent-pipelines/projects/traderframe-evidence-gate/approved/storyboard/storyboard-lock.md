**TraderFrame — Evidence Gate**  
**Storyboard Approval Package**

### **1. Title & Core Message**
**Title:** *TraderFrame — Evidence Gate*  
**Core Message:** *Market conviction becomes credible when evidence is filtered, challenged, approved, and recorded transparently.*

---

### **2. Target Audience**
- **Independent Traders**  
- **Portfolio Managers**  
- **Research-Led Market Participants**  

---

### **3. Brand Principles Integration**
- **Evidence First**  
- **Transparent Reasoning**  
- **User Control**  
- **Risk Awareness**  
- **Calm Precision**  

---

### **4. Experience Overview**
**Format:** Premium Interactive 3D Narrative  
**Length:** 8–10 minutes  
**Tone:** Calm, precise, and intellectually engaging  
**Platform:** Web-based immersive experience (VR/AR supported)  

---

### **5. Storyboard Structure (Time-Based)**

#### **0:00–0:30 – Opening Scene: The Evidence Gate**  
- **Visual:** A sleek, futuristic interface with a glowing "Evidence Gate" logo.  
- **Audio:** Ambient, calm background music with subtle data stream sounds.  
- **Narration (Voiceover):**  
  *"In the world of trading, decisions are made in the blink of an eye. But what if every trade was built on a foundation of evidence?"*  
- **Interactive Element:** User can choose to **open the gate** or **explore the evidence vault**.  

---

#### **0:30–1:30 – Scene 1: The Evidence Vault**  
- **Visual:** A 3D vault with floating data streams, charts, and documents.  
- **Audio:** Data streams and subtle ambient sounds.  
- **Narration (Voiceover):**  
  *"At TraderFrame, we believe that every trade should be grounded in evidence. Here, we gather, filter, and challenge market signals before they become decisions."*  
- **Interactive Element:** User can **select a piece of evidence** (e.g., a news article, chart, or analyst report) to explore.  

---

#### **1:30–3:00 – Scene 2: The Evidence Filter**  
- **Visual:** A 3D filter interface with layers of data.  
- **Audio:** Subtle filtering sounds and data analysis tones.  
- **Narration (Voice, Voiceover):**  
  *"Each piece of evidence is filtered through a rigorous process. We ask: Is this reliable? Is it relevant? Can it be challenged?"*  
- **Interactive Element:** User can **toggle filters** (e.g., time, source, confidence level) to see how evidence is refined.  

---

#### **3:00–4:30 – Scene 3: The Evidence Challenge**  
- **Visual:** A panel where users can **challenge** a data point or signal.  
- **Audio:** Tense, analytical tones as the challenge is processed.  
- **Narration (Voiceover):**  
  *"Even the strongest evidence can be questioned. At TraderFrame, we don’t just accept data—we interrogate it."*  
- **Interactive Element:** User can **submit a challenge** with a reason, and see how the system responds.  

---

#### **4:30–6:00 – Scene 4: The Evidence Approval**  
- **Visual:** A 3D approval panel with a progress bar and confidence meter.  
- **Audio:** A soft, rhythmic tone as the approval process completes.  
- **Narration (Voiceover):**  
  *"Once evidence is filtered and challenged, it’s ready for approval. This is where risk awareness comes into play."*  
- **Interactive Element:** User can **approve** or **reject** a signal based on risk parameters.  

---

#### **6:00–7:30 – Scene 5: The Evidence Record**  
- **Visual:** A secure, transparent ledger where approved evidence is recorded.  
- **Audio:** A soft, confident tone as the record is finalized.  
- **Narration (Voiceover):**  
  *"Every approved signal is recorded transparently. This is your evidence trail—your path to credible market conviction."*  
- **Interactive Element:** User can **view the full record** of approved signals and their reasoning.  

---

#### **7:30–8:30 – Closing Scene: The Trader’s Decision**  
- **Visual:** A trader (avatar) standing at the Evidence Gate, looking at a trading interface.  
- **Audio:** A calm, confident tone.  
- **Narration (Voiceover):**  
  *"With TraderFrame, you don’t just trade—you make decisions with evidence. And that’s how you build conviction in the markets."*  
- **Interactive Element:** User can **make a trade** based on the evidence they’ve reviewed.  

---

### **6. Brand Principle Integration Summary**
| Principle | Integration Point |  
|----------|-------------------|  
| **Evidence First** | Every step of the narrative is built around gathering, filtering, and challenging evidence. |  
| **Transparent Reasoning** | The approval and challenge processes are visualized and explained clearly. |  
| **User Control** | Users can toggle filters, challenge data, and make final decisions. |  
| **Risk Awareness** | Risk parameters are integrated into the approval process. |  
| **Calm Precision** | The tone, visuals, and interactions are designed to be methodical and composed. |
