# Intelligence Language R&D Lab

## Purpose

This branch explores five distinct visual metaphors before any one of them is promoted into the production Experience Engine.

The prototypes are deliberately lightweight. They test semantic fit and visual behavior rather than final rendering quality.

## Shared product question

> Which visual language best communicates TraderFrame turning fragmented market evidence into one explainable, risk-aware decision?

## Candidates

### A. Fiber Intelligence

Signals appear as optical strands. Agreement merges strands, contradiction creates tension or splitting, and aging evidence fades.

### B. Magnetic Fields

Intelligence remains invisible. Its influence bends contour fields, routes, and spatial pressure around evidence sources.

### C. Liquid Probability

Evidence changes viscosity and flow. Contradiction creates interference, risk creates turbulence, and conviction produces a stable current.

### D. Conviction Crystals

Compatible evidence crystallizes into a coherent structure. Weak evidence dissolves and contradiction produces visible fractures.

### E. Topological Intelligence

No individual data object is shown. Reasoning appears as a continuously deforming field where evidence, risk, and contradiction alter topology itself.

## Evaluation criteria

Each direction is scored from 1–5 against:

1. **Clarity** — can viewers understand the state change without a long explanation?
2. **Originality** — does the language avoid familiar AI and fintech clichés?
3. **Scalability** — can it support hero, product, mobile, and editorial uses?
4. **Performance** — can it run responsibly in a real-time browser experience?
5. **Wedge fit** — does it express explainable, risk-aware decision formation?

## Promotion rule

A direction is not selected by score alone. It must also pass these qualitative tests:

- It communicates probability without certainty.
- It keeps contradiction and risk visible.
- It works in motion and in still frames.
- It can evolve into the TraderFrame interface.
- It has enough ownability to become a recognizable brand behavior.

## Current strategic hypothesis

Topological Intelligence and Magnetic Fields have the strongest relationship to Conviction Atlas. Fiber Intelligence remains the clearest fallback or supporting layer. Liquid Probability and Conviction Crystals should be considered for specific moments rather than automatically becoming the entire system.

## Next decision

After browser review, select one of three outcomes:

1. Promote one direction unchanged.
2. Create a hybrid with one primary language and one supporting behavior.
3. Reject all five and open another R&D round.
