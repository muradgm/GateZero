# TraderFrame Creative System

## Purpose

This system governs how TraderFrame concepts move from product meaning to production.

The process is deliberately split into two layers:

1. **Creative Thinking** — determine what the experience means and why it should exist.
2. **Production** — determine how the approved concept is designed, built, tested, and shipped.

No production role should begin asset creation before the Creative Thinking layer is approved.

## Product definition

TraderFrame is a private trading decision-intelligence platform. It interprets market structure, events, sentiment, flows, technical evidence, and risk context to form an explainable directional thesis and recommend whether the user should act, wait, reduce exposure, or reject the setup.

## Wedge

> TraderFrame turns fragmented market evidence into one explainable, risk-aware trading decision.

## Concept direction

**Conviction Atlas** maps possible market outcomes as a changing landscape. Evidence raises or weakens paths, contradiction splits them, risk closes unsafe routes, and one recommendation emerges without removing uncertainty or user control.

## Workflow

```text
Creative Thinking

Production

├─ Design System

├─ Motion & Camera
├─ Shader & VFX
├─ Implementation
├─ QA & Optimization
└─ Deployment
```
