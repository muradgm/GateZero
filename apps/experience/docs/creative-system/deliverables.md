## Asset budgets — initial targets

- Initial JavaScript under 250 KB gzip before the 3D runtime
- 3D runtime lazy-loaded
- Hero GLB under 2.5 MB compressed
- Textures under 4 MB total on desktop and 1.5 MB on mobile
- Fewer than 80 active draw calls in the hero state
- Stable 45–60 FPS on a typical modern laptop
- Stable 30 FPS minimum on supported mobile devices
- Reduced-motion mode without continuous animation

These are starting constraints and must be validated against the final authored scene.
