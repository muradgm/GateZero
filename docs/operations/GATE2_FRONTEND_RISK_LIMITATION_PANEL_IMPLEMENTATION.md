# Gate 2 Frontend Risk Limitation Panel Implementation

## Summary

TRD-494 implements read-only risk and limitation panels.

## Implemented Surface

- Local-only data limitation.
- Paper-simulation planning limitation.
- Evidence-before-action limitation.
- Operator-owned decision limitation.
- External-account, execution, prediction, and claim-surface risk notes.

## Boundary

The panels are claim-safe and do not imply approval, readiness, safety, profitability, or execution
authority.
