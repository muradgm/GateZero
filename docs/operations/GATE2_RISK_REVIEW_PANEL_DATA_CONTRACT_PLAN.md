# Gate 2 Risk-Review Panel Data Contract Plan

TRD: TRD-514

## Plan

Risk-review panel data should separate risk checks, blockers, assumptions, limitation references,
and review notes. It must not collapse those fields into approval status.

## Boundary

Risk review is evidence, not permission.
