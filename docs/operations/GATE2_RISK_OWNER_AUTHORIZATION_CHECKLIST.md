# Gate 2 Risk Owner Authorization Checklist

TRD-395 drafts required risk-owner checks before any future gate movement.

## Checklist

- Confirm risk gates are equal or stricter.
- Confirm no test or backtest result grants trading permission.
- Confirm strategy reports remain evidence-only.
- Confirm operator decision authority remains required.
- Confirm execution scope is separately reviewed.

## Decision

This checklist does not approve Gate 2 or loosen any risk gate.
