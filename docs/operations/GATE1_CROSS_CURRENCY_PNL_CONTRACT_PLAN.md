# Gate 1 Cross-Currency PnL Contract Plan

## Purpose

Plan cross-currency PnL evidence requirements for Gate 1 historical backtesting.

## Required Evidence

- Instrument and quote currency.
- Account currency.
- Conversion rate into account currency.
- Gross quote-currency PnL.
- Gross account-currency PnL.
- Declared costs and net account-currency PnL.

## Boundary

The plan does not authorize live data, account connections, execution, strategy approval, readiness,
or performance claims.
