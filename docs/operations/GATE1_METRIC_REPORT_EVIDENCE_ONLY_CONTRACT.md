# Gate 1 Metric Report Evidence-Only Contract

## Purpose

Define the local metric report evidence contract for Gate 1 historical backtesting.

## Requirements

- Metrics are evidence-only.
- Sample size must be explicit.
- Limitation notes are required.
- Profitability labels and performance claims are rejected.

## Boundary

Metric reports do not approve, rank, promote, or certify strategies. They remain local evidence for
operator and risk review.
