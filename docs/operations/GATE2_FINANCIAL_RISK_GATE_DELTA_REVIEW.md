# Gate 2 Financial Risk Gate Delta Review

TRD-388 reviews financial-risk-gate deltas required before any future phase can be considered.

## Required Deltas

- Formal risk packet for any paper or simulated execution semantics.
- Explicit distinction between backtest evidence and execution permission.
- No strategy approval, readiness, profitability, or safety claims.
- Operator decision authority remains required.
- New controls must be equal or stricter than current controls.

## Decision

Financial risk gates are not loosened. Gate 2 remains blocked.
