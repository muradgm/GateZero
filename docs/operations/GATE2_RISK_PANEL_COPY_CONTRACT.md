# Gate 2 Risk Panel Copy Contract

## Summary

TRD-472 defines future risk panel language.

## Required Labels

- Risk review recorded.
- Blocker present.
- Evidence incomplete.
- Manual operator review required.
- Local paper-simulation evidence only.

## Blocked Labels

- Approved.
- Ready.
- Safe to trade.
- Certified.
- Passed for execution.
- Promoted.
- Deployable.

## Result

Risk panels must show risk state and blockers without becoming approval surfaces.
