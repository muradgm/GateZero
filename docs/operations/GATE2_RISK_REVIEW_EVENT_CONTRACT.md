# Gate 2 Risk Review Event Contract

TRD-426 adds the Gate 2 risk review event contract schema.

## Required Boundaries

- Operator acknowledgement is required.
- Blocked dispositions require blocking issues.
- Risk events remain control records.
- Risk events do not approve live, external, automated, or strategy-performance behavior.
