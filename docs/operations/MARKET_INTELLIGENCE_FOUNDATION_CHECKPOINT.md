# Market Intelligence Foundation Checkpoint

## Checkpoint Question

Does the foundation support a useful, source-linked, read-only intelligence brief without creating
trading authority?

## Evidence

- A governed analyst role defines advisory authority and refusal behavior.
- Local replay sources require canonical tracked repository files with verified SHA-256 hashes.
- Hourly, daily, and monthly evidence remain separate.
- Freshness is derived, provenance is structured, and evidence and semantic quality fail closed.
- Scenario synthesis emits bullish, bearish, and neutral scenarios together or emits none.
- Scenario synthesis rescans every rendered field and enforces evidence-chain identity.
- Adversarial review blocks placeholder, unlinked, or untestable scenario evidence.
- Historical replay derives control rates from linked frozen artifacts without performance claims.
- The manual workflow keeps risk and operator decisions outside the analyst.

## Verdict

Conditionally supported after full repository verification and independent RISK and QA_SECURITY
review.

## Authorized Next Packet

If those reviews pass, `TRD-757` may implement one local, read-only Intelligence Brief MVP using the
accepted contracts and fixtures.

TRD-757 must not add:

- network sources or scheduled scanning
- providers, credentials, accounts, or broker routes
- trade instructions or opportunity ranking
- final recommendations or autonomous decisions
- simulated-order dispatch or live execution
- profitability, edge, readiness, approval, or promotion claims
- export, share, print, alert, or publishing channels

## Stop Condition

Any failed source, semantic, timeframe, adversarial, risk, or QA check keeps TRD-757 blocked and
routes the defect into a remediation packet.
