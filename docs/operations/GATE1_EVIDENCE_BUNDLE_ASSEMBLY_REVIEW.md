# Gate 1 Evidence Bundle Assembly Review

## Purpose

Review the Gate 1 evidence bundle assembly posture after assembly, metrics, risk, reproducibility,
and operator decision contracts were added.

## Review Result

The evidence bundle remains local, evidence-only, and risk-gated. Gate 2 remains blocked until
separate paper-trading controls and approvals exist.

## Boundary

The review does not make strategy performance claims, does not approve a strategy, and does not
authorize paper or live execution.
