# TraderFrame Identity Alternative - Signal Window

## Core Idea

Signal Window presents TraderFrame as a disciplined review window: the trade idea is framed, the
signal is contained, and the approval axis stays visually central.

This direction is less literal than a TF monogram. It is more product-system oriented and could work
well if the brand should feel like a control surface rather than a personal mark.

## Symbol

- Deep-purple corner frame: the bounded research workspace.
- Violet signal rail: market information contained inside the frame.
- Teal approval axis: risk approval and operator control.
- Small central node: the decision point before action.

## Best Use

Use this direction if TraderFrame should feel calm, modern, and analytical, with less emphasis on
initials and more emphasis on the review process.

## Tradeoffs

- Strong as an app icon and UI mark.
- Less immediately letter-like than a TF monogram.
- More distinctive as a product-system symbol than as a personal signature.
