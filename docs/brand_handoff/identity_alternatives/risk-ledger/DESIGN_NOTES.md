# TraderFrame Identity Alternative - Risk Ledger

## Core Idea

Risk Ledger presents TraderFrame as an evidence ledger for trading decisions: every strategy, risk
assumption, and operator decision is recorded before action.

This direction is more operational and institutional than Signal Window. It is well suited if the
product should feel like a research-control system with audit discipline.

## Symbol

- Deep-purple ledger frame: the controlled record surface.
- Three vertical bars: evidence, risk, and decision columns.
- Violet execution rail: action remains subordinate to the record.
- Teal approval column: risk-cleared decision state.

## Best Use

Use this direction if TraderFrame should feel more serious, internal, audit-grade, and risk-office
adjacent.

## Tradeoffs

- Strong for dashboards and command-center surfaces.
- Less expressive than Signal Window.
- More mature and restrained, but slightly less memorable as a consumer-facing app icon.
