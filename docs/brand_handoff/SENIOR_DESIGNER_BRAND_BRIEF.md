# TraderFrame Brand Identity Brief

## Project

TraderFrame needs a refined logo and brand identity system for a serious personal trading research,
risk-control, and execution-support product.

This brief is for a senior brand identity designer. The goal is to create a premium, credible,
production-ready identity system, not a quick AI-generated logo concept.

## About TraderFrame

TraderFrame is a personal trading operating system for one serious trader/operator.

It helps the operator research strategies, review evidence, frame risk, journal decisions, and
support disciplined execution workflows.

TraderFrame is not a trading signal seller, not a casino-style trading app, not a crypto hype
product, and not an AI money machine.

## Core Wedge

> Research the trade. Frame the risk. Execute only when allowed.

This is the central idea of the brand.

TraderFrame should communicate that trading decisions must be framed by research, evidence, risk
approval, and operator judgment before execution.

## Service Definition

TraderFrame supports strategy research, evidence review, backtest interpretation, risk framing,
trade journaling, execution readiness checks, approval gates, and disciplined decision support.

The product should feel like a calm operating system for trading discipline, not a tool that
promises market prediction or profit.

## Brand Meaning

TraderFrame means framing every trade before action: evidence before execution, risk visibility
before permission, research before action, operator control over automation, and discipline over
excitement.

## Desired Brand Personality

The brand should feel disciplined, precise, intelligent, calm, serious, premium, trustworthy,
operator-grade, evidence-first, and risk-aware.

The brand must not feel hype-driven, casino-like, childish, aggressive, mascot-like, generic AI
SaaS, get-rich-quick, or profit-promising.

## Preferred Logo Direction

The preferred direction is a strong TF monogram integrated with frame, threshold, decision-spine,
and risk-control symbolism.

Symbol meaning:

- TF: TraderFrame and product identity
- Frame: structured review before execution
- Decision spine: operator judgment and control path
- Risk boundary: containment before action
- Execution bar: controlled permission to proceed

The final mark should be simple. It should work as an app icon, favicon, product navigation mark,
and full brand lockup.

## Visual Direction

Preferred visual language:

- deep purple authority
- violet controlled-action accents
- teal approved-state accents
- restrained signal orange accents
- slate light surfaces
- sharp vector geometry
- geometric typography
- strong silhouette
- monochrome-first discipline
- premium fintech restraint

Suggested palette:

- Deep Purple: `#1A1028`
- Violet: `#7C5CFF`
- Teal: `#00C6A7`
- Slate: `#EAEFF5`
- Signal Orange: `#FF8A00`

## Typography Direction

The wordmark should feel geometric, sharp, modern, serious, slightly wide, premium fintech, and
readable at small sizes.

Preferred primary type direction:

- Raleway SemiBold / Bold / ExtraBold

Preferred secondary type direction:

- Inter for body text, UI, labels, and data

Avoid default unedited fonts, sci-fi fonts, overly rounded startup fonts, stencil typography, and
sports-style lettering.

## Messaging Direction

Potential tagline territory:

- Research the trade. Frame the risk. Execute only when allowed.
- Evidence in. Risk framed. Execution controlled.
- The control surface for disciplined trading decisions.
- Frame the risk before the trade.
- Research first. Risk always.

Messaging should emphasize control, evidence, discipline, risk approval, and operator judgment.

Messaging must not imply guaranteed returns, easy money, predictive certainty, or automated trading
success.

## Required Deliverables

Provide a complete identity system, including primary stacked logo lockup, horizontal logo lockup,
symbol-only mark, wordmark-only version, app icon, favicon, monochrome black version, monochrome
white version, dark-background version, light-background version, small-size simplified mark, color
palette, typography recommendation, clear-space rules, minimum-size rules, incorrect usage examples,
SVG exports, PNG exports, and Figma source file.

## Final Direction

The strongest identity will feel like a professional-grade trading control system: structured, calm,
precise, premium, and disciplined.

The brand should make the operator feel in control before execution, not excited by the promise of
profit.
