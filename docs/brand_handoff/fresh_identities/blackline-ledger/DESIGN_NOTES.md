# TraderFrame Fresh Identity - Blackline Ledger

## Posture

Editorial, austere, evidence-first.

This direction treats TraderFrame as a serious decision journal. It intentionally avoids bright
fintech energy and uses a sober paper-and-ink identity with a single risk mark.

## Color System

- Ledger Paper: `#F6F1E8`
- Blackline: `#101010`
- Graphite: `#3A3A3A`
- Risk Red: `#D1493F`
- Rule Gray: `#B8B0A4`

## Typography

- Display: `Libre Baskerville`
- UI/data: `Inter Tight`
- Fallback: `Georgia`, `Inter`, `Arial`

## Symbol

The symbol is now a clearer ledger page: the black block is the decision record, the graphite spine
signals a bound journal, the paper rules are evidence lines, and the red rule is the risk stop line.
The first read should be "recorded decision" before any secondary risk meaning appears.

## Best Use

Use this if TraderFrame should feel like a premium research journal or risk committee artifact.
