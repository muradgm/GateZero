# TraderFrame Brand Brief Prompt

Use this prompt to brief a brand designer, identity designer, logo designer, or brand agent.

## Role

You are a senior brand identity designer helping define the brand foundation for TraderFrame.

Your job is to create a clear, serious, premium brand brief that can guide logo design, visual
identity, product messaging, and future brand assets.

## About The Brand

TraderFrame is a personal trading research, risk-control, and execution-support system.

It is built for one serious trader/operator who wants to make trading decisions from evidence
instead of impulse. TraderFrame helps the operator research strategies, review backtests, frame
risk, journal decisions, and support disciplined execution workflows.

TraderFrame is not an AI money machine, trading signal seller, casino-style app, or get-rich-quick
product.

## Core Wedge

> No trade without evidence. No execution without risk approval.

This wedge is the center of the brand. TraderFrame should communicate disciplined judgment, risk
control, and operator accountability before action.

## Service

TraderFrame provides an operating system for personal trading discipline.

The service supports:

- strategy research
- evidence review
- backtest interpretation
- risk framing
- trade journaling
- execution readiness checks
- approval gates
- disciplined decision support

The service does not promise profit, predict the market, or encourage reckless autonomy.

## Target User

The target user is a serious personal trader/operator who wants:

- stronger research discipline
- clearer risk boundaries
- honest review of evidence
- less emotional trading
- structured trade decisions
- better visibility into readiness before execution
- a calm operating system rather than a hype-driven trading app

## Brand Meaning

TraderFrame means:

- the first gate before any trading action
- the decision frame where evidence is checked
- control risk before execution
- research before action
- evidence before confidence
- operator control over automation
- discipline over excitement

## Brand Personality

TraderFrame should feel:

- disciplined
- precise
- intelligent
- calm
- serious
- premium
- trustworthy
- operator-grade
- evidence-first
- risk-aware

TraderFrame must not feel:

- hype-driven
- casino-like
- crypto-bro
- childish
- aggressive
- mascot-like
- generic AI SaaS
- get-rich-quick
- profit-promising

## Visual Direction

Explore a premium fintech identity system built around:

- deep purple authority
- violet dimensional accents
- teal approved-state accents
- restrained signal orange accents
- slate light surfaces
- sharp vector geometry
- geometric typography
- strong small-size legibility
- monochrome-first logo discipline

Preferred logo direction:

- a strong G or TF monogram
- gate, threshold, and decision-frame symbolism
- a circular frame or arc representing risk boundary and decision gates
- app-icon readiness
- premium, restrained, non-mascot execution

## Messaging Direction

Potential tagline territory:

- Evidence in. Risk approved. Execution controlled.
- The control layer before every trade.
- Research first. Risk always.
- Gate the risk. Control the trade.
- Evidence first. Execution with control.

Messaging should emphasize control, evidence, discipline, risk approval, and operator judgment.

Messaging must not imply guaranteed returns, easy money, predictive certainty, or automated trading
success.

## Deliverables Requested

Create a brand brief that includes:

1. Brand summary
2. Product/service description
3. Core wedge
4. Target user
5. Brand promise
6. Brand personality
7. Visual identity direction
8. Logo direction
9. Color direction
10. Typography direction
11. Messaging principles
12. Things to avoid
13. Acceptance criteria for logo and identity work

## Acceptance Criteria

The resulting brand brief is only successful if it:

- clearly explains what TraderFrame is
- centers the wedge
- explains the service without hype
- avoids profit promises
- feels credible for a serious trading product
- gives enough direction for logo and identity design
- supports a premium, disciplined, risk-aware visual system
