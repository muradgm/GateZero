# TraderFrame Brand System

## Brand Role

TraderFrame is the active product identity for the trading research, risk-control, and
execution-support workspace.

The brand should make one idea immediately clear:

> Research the trade. Frame the risk. Execute only when allowed.

Former-name material is historical exploration unless it is explicitly marked as legacy reference.

## Brand Essence

TraderFrame helps one serious operator turn trading ideas into reviewed, risk-bounded decisions.

It should feel like a disciplined trading control surface: calm, precise, evidence-led, and
operator-controlled.

## Positioning

TraderFrame is a personal trading research, risk-control, and execution-support system.

It helps the operator:

- research strategies honestly
- frame risk before action
- review evidence and assumptions
- interpret backtests without hype
- preserve decision traceability
- move from research to paper trading only when the evidence supports it

TraderFrame is not an AI money machine, signal seller, broker platform, casino-like trading app, or
guaranteed-profit product.

## Visual Identity

The logo is a simple `TF` frame mark built from a trade frame, decision spine, risk boundary, and
controlled execution bar.

Use `logo_exports/traderframe-symbol.svg` for the symbol and
`logo_exports/traderframe-horizontal-lockup.svg` for most product surfaces.

Use `logo_exports/traderframe-app-icon.svg` for app icons.

## Color System

| Token            | Hex       | Role                                                |
| ---------------- | --------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Deep Purple      | `#1A1028` | Primary brand authority, dark surfaces, symbol core |
| Blackened Purple | `#09040F` | Deep mode backgrounds and icon depth                |
| Violet           | `#7C5CFF` | Execution bar, focused action, active frame         |
| Teal             | `#00C6A7` | Approved state, risk-cleared state, decision spine  |
| Slate            | `#EAEFF5` | Light surfaces and quiet UI panels                  |
| Signal Orange    | `#FF8A00` | Warnings and attention states only                  |

Use violet and teal sparingly. Risk, evidence, and review state should guide color use.

## Typography

- Brand/display: Raleway SemiBold, Bold, or ExtraBold
- UI/body/data: Inter
- Direction: geometric, serious, optically tight, and readable at small sizes

## Voice

TraderFrame speaks with restraint:

- evidence before opinion
- risk before reward
- blocked is a valid safety outcome
- execution is controlled, never hyped

Good phrases:

- Research the trade. Frame the risk. Execute only when allowed.
- Evidence in. Risk framed. Execution controlled.
- The control surface for disciplined trading decisions.
- Blocked by risk.
- Needs more evidence.

Avoid:

- Winning strategy
- Guaranteed edge
- AI says buy
- Safe profit
- Passive income
- Set and forget

## UI Application

The brand posture for the app is dense and operational, not landing-page glossy.

Use the brand to improve evidence freshness, risk verdicts, source links, review coverage, operator
handoff clarity, and strategy maturity state.

Do not use the brand to imply live-trading readiness or performance confidence.

## Clear Space

For the symbol, keep clear space at least equal to the width of the teal decision spine on all
sides.

For lockups, keep clear space at least equal to the symbol's internal frame width.

## Minimum Sizes

- Favicon symbol: 16px minimum
- Product nav symbol: 24px minimum
- App icon: 64px minimum in previews
- Horizontal lockup: 180px minimum width
- Primary stacked lockup: 140px minimum width

Below 24px, use the simplified small symbol or favicon.

## Incorrect Usage

Do not add upward arrows or candlesticks, bevels, glows, fake 3D, profit symbolism, low-contrast
chart backgrounds, or claims that imply guaranteed performance.
