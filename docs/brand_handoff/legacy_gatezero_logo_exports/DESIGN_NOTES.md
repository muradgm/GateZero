# GateZero Logo Draft - Controlled Judgment

## Strategic Read

The mark is built around a restrained TF monogram inside a partial control arc. The falcon is
implied through a sharp negative-space cut rather than rendered as an illustrated animal, keeping
the identity closer to premium fintech than sports or mascot branding.

## Logo Concept

- `T / TF`: structure, decision framing, and product identity.
- `Falcon profile`: focus, review, and disciplined judgment.
- `Arc`: risk boundary and execution gate.
- `Teal accent`: control state.
- `Orange accent`: signal, used sparingly.

## Production Notes

- Wordmark is still live SVG text and should be outlined in final production exports.
- The symbol uses SVG masks for the falcon cutout. Flatten/boolean the paths in Figma or Illustrator
  before final handoff.
- Recommended type direction: Space Grotesk, Sora, or a custom geometric sans wordmark with adjusted
  `A`, `R`, and `F` spacing.
- Minimum recommended sizes: full symbol 24px, small symbol 16px, horizontal lockup 180px wide,
  stacked lockup 220px wide.
- Figma source and PDF exports are not created in this draft pass.
